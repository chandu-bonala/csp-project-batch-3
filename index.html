Introduction

Ethics and human values are guiding principles that help individuals decide what is right and wrong. They shape our decisions, relationships, and society itself. Understanding and practicing them builds trust, harmony, and a fair world.

Detailed Information

Ethics, Morals, & Values

Ethics are rules of conduct; morals are personal principles; values are beliefs that guide our decisions.

Right vs. Wrong

Right actions align with moral principles and benefit others. Wrong actions cause harm. Example: Returning a lost wallet vs. keeping it.

Core Topics

Honesty & Integrity

Being truthful in all situations. Example: Gandhi refusing to cheat in school.

Activity: Role-play returning lost money.
Respect for Others

Respecting teachers, classmates, and cultural diversity.

Activity: Share customs from different cultures.

Extra Insights

Types of Ethics

Normative, Applied, and Meta-ethics explain morality and its role in society.

Benefits in Life

Encourages cooperation, fairness, and strengthens relationships.

Real-world Examples

Helping disaster victims, returning lost items, protecting the environment.

Conclusion & Pledge

By practicing ethics and human values, we create a society that is fair, kind, and just. I pledge to uphold these values in my daily life.
